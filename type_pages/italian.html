Vapiano

What is Vapianos

Vapiano make handmade pasta, freshly made pizza dough, & salads alongside the best buffalo mozzarella, meats & cheese antipasti plate.

Their restaurants are spacious with some seating looking over their open kitchen. A 100-year-old olive trees as part of the decor & fresh live herbs pots on every table. It offers diners a unique concept - with guests enjoying face-to-face interactions with chefs as they cook their dish to order, exactly as the guest likes it. Extra chilli? Sure. No garlic? No problem. Extra Chicken or King Prawns, easy!

Guests are given a chip card on arrival, using it to order both drinks and food. Your card registers everything ordered, you simply pay on the way out. One card each meaning everyone pays for what they order, avoiding the hassle of splitting bills which is great for large groups, dates and students!
