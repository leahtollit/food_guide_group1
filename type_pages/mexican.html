Wahaca

Award winning street food

Housed over 2 floors in the city’s historic Corn Exchange building on Exchange Square, Wahaca Manchester brings award winning street food to this bustling dining quarter in the heart of Manchester. Drop in for a drink in our Atrium cocktail bar, where we will be shaking fresh blends with a modern Mexican twist.
