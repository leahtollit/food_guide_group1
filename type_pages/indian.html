Scene Indian Street Kitchen

What is Scene?

Airy, contemporary space with wood and designer lamps, for Indo-Chinese street food-inspired dishes.

Lunch Menu

Whether you are relaxing during your work break or grabbing a snack mid-shopping trip our lunch menu has a wide selection of traditional street dishes that are sure to satisfy.

Evening Menu

Bringing the exotic flavours of the subcontinent to Manchester. Experience the freshness and vibrancy of Asia with our finely crafted menu using only the freshest of ingredients and traditionally trained chefs with a passion for food that truly shows in our dishes.

Scene offer various Hot and Cold drinks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic to fit everyones taste. They also offer desserts to those with a sweet tooth.

Another feature of this restaurant is the sisha bar, which operates on a first come first serve basis.

Popular Dishes

Samosa Chaat

Special pastry filled with spiced potato and peas then topped with chickpeas and drizzled with an assortment of chutneys amd sweet yoghurt. Garnished with sev and pomegranite.

Nihari

Lamb shank cooked with garlic, ginger, yoghurt, onion, tomatoes, wheat flour and spices.

Dhaba Murgh

A Punjab favourite. Chicken cooked in a thick sauce with peppersm spring onions, fresh coriander and roasted spices.
