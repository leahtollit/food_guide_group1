Wholesome Junkies

Wholesome Junk Food!

Wholesome Junkies - 100% Vegan junk food with a wholesome twist. We are passionate foodies who want to show everyone that vegan food is not bland or boring. Ditch those falafel balls and quinoa, we are talking about Kentucky Fried Cauliflower Wings, Buffalo Mac 'n' Cheese and Doner Kebabs.

Our drive is to try and save the planet one meal at a time, and to enlighten people with new and creative ways to eat in a more sustainable way.
