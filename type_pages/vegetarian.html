Wholesome Junkies

Wholesome Junk Food!

Located in the south side of the Anrdale food market, Wholefood Junkies offers vegan and vegetarian fast food. Anything from Chickn Burgers, Beefless Burgers and Hotdogs but all 100% vegan!

Thier restaurants are spacious with some seating looking over thier open kitchen. A 100-year-old olive trees as part of the decor & fresh live herbs pots on every table. It offers diners a unique concept - with guests enjoying face-to-face interactions with chefs as they cook their dish to order, exactly as the guest likes it. Extra chilli? Sure. No garlic? No problem. Extra Chicken or King Prawns, easy!

Guests are given a chip card on arrival, using it to order both drinks and food. Your card registers everything ordered, you simply pay on the way out. One card each meaning everyone pays for what they order, avoiding the hassle of splitting bills which is great for large groups, dates and students!
