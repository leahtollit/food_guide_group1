About Wagamama

Wagamama is a chain restauraunt that first opened in London in 1992. They operate following the motto of 'Kaizen' which when translated means 'good change'. Wagamama found its inspiration from traditional fast-paced Japanese ramen bars and a celebration of Asian food. They set out to create a unique way of eating and bringing the fresh, nourishing flavours of Asia to all. A passage from the wagamama website describes their food as 'A little ginger to warm you up. chillies that kick. noodles to nourish the body and soul. our asian inspired menu has been created to soothe, nourish, sustain and inspire'.
