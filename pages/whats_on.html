What's On

Manchester gin Festival

September 25-26th 2020

Proud to be a festival for everyone, the Manchester Gin Festival stocks a range of more than 15 premium Rums, from the delectable dark to the specially spiced and the fantastically flavoured. To help set the party mood there will also be amazing live music, fabulous food and of course loads of fun. Sessions:

  1. Friday Evening: 6.30pm to 11pm
  2. Saturday Afternoon: 12noon to 4pm
  3. Saturday Evening: 6.30pm to 11pm

Our festival is strictly for over 18s – no babies or children will be allowed. The Gin Society encourages you to drink responsibly.

Manchester Cathedral, Victoria Street, Manchester, M3 1SX

Tickets from £12.50

Northern Vegan Festival

Saturday 23rd May 2020 10:30am – 16:30pm

One of the UK's largest vegan festivals with 100+ stalls, world food caterers, exciting cookery demos, inspirational talks, interactive workshops, yoga and children's activities running throughout the day.100% of ticket money goes to animal welfare charities as the festival is fully volunteer run.

Bowlers Exhibition Centre, Longbridge Road, Stretford, M17 1EH

Pay on the door or book from £5
