Explore Greater Manchester's diverse culinary scene

Manchester’s food and drink culture is continuing to thrive. With new restaurants, bars and cafes opening weekly, the city is establishing itself as one of the UK’s most exciting foodie destinations.

Why not try the diverse range of eateries in the Corn Exchange, or experience local produce from many of Greater Manchester's independent and quirky coffee shops? This city is a haven for vegan and vegetarian cuisine and is fit to burst with a number of halal options too. .
